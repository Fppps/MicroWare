FAQ
Will it stay free?

We intend to keep a generous free tier while we learn from usage. If paid tiers arrive, the free tier will still cover core features.

Any usage limits?

Reasonable rate limits may apply (to keep the lights on and service stable). We’ll publish specifics with 2-lite.

Can I use medical outputs?

They’re strictly educational. Always consult a licensed professional before making health decisions.

Roadmap & Principles

We ship in steps. Each release expands capability while keeping trust and clarity front-and-center.

Plain-English, disciplined descriptions

Every Girare AI card states what it does—and what it doesn’t. Health content remains strictly informational. The “regenerate” quirk persists in 1.x lines and is disclosed up front to avoid surprises.
