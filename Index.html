About MicWin

MicWin is an experimental intelligence built by MicroWare. We’re prioritizing a strong knowledge substrate inside the model’s codebase, but we’re deliberately holding off on defining a single purpose. This gives us room to discover authentic use‑cases through research and real signals rather than forcing a destination too early.

What we know today

The internal architecture is being tuned for fast retrieval, safe tool use, and transparent reasoning surfaces. Everything else remains flexible.

What we don’t know yet

The ultimate purpose. MicWin’s direction will be shaped by prototyping, evaluation, and community feedback.

Design principle

“Purpose emerges from capability.” We grow capabilities first, then validate purpose against real problems.

FAQ

Is MicWin competing with NovaAI?

MicWin explores a different path. As stated above, “It challenges NovaAI with knowledge within the AI’s code.” That statement reflects a focus on deeply‑integrated knowledge rather than feature parity.

Can I try it today?

Not yet. The demo button is disabled until we reach the Closed Preview stage.

What is the purpose?

Intentionally undecided. We’re letting real capability and research findings guide the purpose.
