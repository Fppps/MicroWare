MicWin 2 Roadmap

Planned direction for the next major release. Subject to change as we learn from users.

  • More knowledge for astrophysics
    Focus areas include exoplanets, stellar evolution, orbital mechanics, cosmology summaries, and data-aware explanations suited for beginners to enthusiasts.
  • More options for thinking
    Selectable “Thinking Modes” (e.g., Concise, Step-by-Step, Planner) to match depth vs. speed, with clearer rationales and intermediate steps.
  • Video Link Testing
    Validate video URLs, auto-preview metadata (title, duration, source), and test playback compatibility—all without storing user content.
Privacy & safety (planned)

Video Link Testing will operate on user-provided links, avoid uploading content, and will show what checks were performed. Astrophysics content will cite public sources where appropriate. Thinking Modes will display rationale summaries for transparency.
